Hello fellow virtual pilots!

I would like to share a completely free tool I developed for the community to help quickly calculate landing wind components (Crosswind/Tailwind) during our flights, especially on the VATSIM network.

Introducing Airbus Wind Calc: You just enter your arrival ICAO, and the app fetches live METAR data directly from VATSIM. It then calculates the wind components and checks them against the specific operational limits of your selected aircraft and engine type (A320neo/ceo CFM/IAE/PW, A321, A330).

You can install it on your iPad or smartphone to use it just like a native full-screen EFB app, without any annoying browser toolbars. No need for the App Store or Google Play!

📱 How to Install as a Mobile App (PWA):

🍏 For iOS (iPhone & iPad):

    Open the link above using the Safari browser.

    Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the square with an arrow pointing up 📤).

    Scroll down the menu and tap "Add to Home Screen".

    Tap "Add" in the top right corner. The app is now on your home screen!

🤖 For Android:

    Open the link above using the Chrome browser.

    Tap the "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" prompt that pops up at the bottom of the screen. (If it doesn't appear, tap the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner and select "Add to Home Screen" from the menu).

    Confirm, and you're good to go!
